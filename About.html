Eventura Music & Arts Collective

About the Organization:

Eventura Music & Arts Collective is a community-driven organization based in Bangalore, dedicated to promoting music, art, and culture. Established in 2015, Eventura has become a well-known platform for emerging and established artists to showcase their talent. With a focus on organizing lively events that blend music, food, and creativity, the collective aims to create memorable experiences for people of all ages.

Mission:

To bring together artists, musicians, and communities to celebrate the diverse cultural spirit of Bangalore through inclusive, accessible, and unforgettable events.
